Origin of Takoyaki

Takoyaki was invented in Osaka, Japan, in the 1930s by street vendor Tomekichi Endo. It's now a beloved street food across Japan and the world.

The Perfect Cooking Technique

Takoyaki requires a special griddle with round molds to achieve its signature shape. Cooks masterfully flip the batter with skewers to ensure golden perfection.

Traditional Toppings

Classic takoyaki is topped with savory takoyaki sauce, Japanese mayo, bonito flakes, and aonori seaweed. The bonito flakes appear to "dance" when sprinkled on hot takoyaki!
